About BLEZEL ART

Graphite. Soul. Story.

I’m Blessings Matika, a graphite pencil artist from Blantyre. Every line I draw is about capturing emotion, identity, and the quiet power in everyday people.

BLEZEL ART started as a sketchbook in school and grew into portraits that speak. No color, no distractions. Just raw graphite and truth.

I specialize in custom portraits, commissions, and pieces that turn memories into art you can hold.
